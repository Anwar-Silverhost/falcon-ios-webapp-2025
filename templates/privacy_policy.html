Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 28 March 2024

Welcome to Rotary Wing EFB Solution. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle information when you use our app.

Introduction

Rotary Wing EFB Solution is designed to assist helicopter pilots with essential flight tools. We prioritize data security and user privacy, ensuring minimal data collection and no unnecessary tracking.

Information We Collect

Our app does not collect personal data. However, certain functionalities may require access to limited data:

A. Automatically Collected Data

When using specific app features, we may temporarily collect:

  • Device Information: Model, operating system, and app version for performance optimization.
  • Location Data: Used for navigation and situational awareness but never stored or shared.
  • Log Data: If the app crashes, we collect non-personal diagnostic reports to improve functionality.
B. Data You Provide
  • Flight-related inputs: Data entered for calculations, planning, or checklists remains on your device.
  • Document Uploads: Any files uploaded for in-app reference stay locally on your device and are not transmitted externally.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information only for the following purposes:

  • To enhance app performance and user experience
  • To enable essential flight functionalities, such as navigation and weather updates
  • To maintain security and troubleshoot issues

Data Security

Your data security is our top priority.

  • The app is designed to work offline, reducing internet-based vulnerabilities.
  • We use industry-standard encryption where applicable.
  • Any third-party integrations are limited to aviation data providers (e.g.,weather services).

Third-Party Services

While our app does not collect personal data, we use external services for aviation-related updates. These services operate independently and may have their own privacy policies.

Data Retention & Deletion

Since we do not store personal data, there is no need for user account deletion requests. All flight-related data remains on your device unless manually deleted by you.

Your Rights & Choices

  • You can disable location services at any time via device settings.
  • You may delete local data by clearing the app storage.
  • If any personal data handling policy changes, users will be notified.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Significant changes will be communicated within the app or via an update notice.
