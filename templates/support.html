About Rotary Wing EFB Solution

Rotary Wing EFB Solution is an advanced Electronic Flight Bag (EFB) designed specifically for helicopter pilots. It provides tools for flight planning, navigation, weather updates, performance calculations, and document management — all in one place.

Getting Started
  • Download & Install – Get the app from the App Store.
  • Grant Permissions – Allow location access for navigation features.
  • Set Up Your Flight Tools – Configure charts, weather updates, and calculations.
  • Use Offline Mode or Online Mode – Download necessary documents for offline use.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Can I use the app offline?

Yes! You can store flight charts and documents for offline use. Simply download the required files in advance.

Does the app require an account?

Yes, Rotary Wing EFB Solution require user accounts and logins.

Is my flight data stored on your servers?

No, all flight-related data is stored locally on your device for privacy and security.

  • Issue: The app is not showing my location.

    • Ensure location services are enabled (see FAQ #1).
    • Restart your device and relaunch the app.
    • Check if your device is in Airplane Mode (disable it).

  • Issue: Flight planning tools are not loading.

    • Make sure you have a stable internet connection.
    • Restart the app and check for updates.

  • Issue: The app crashes on startup.

    • Ensure you are using the latest app version.
    • If the issue persists, reinstall the app.
